What is TKR HACK CONQUEST 2.0?

TKR HACK CONQUEST 2.0 is a 24-Hour National Level Hackathon organized by the IEEE Student Branch, TKR College of Engineering & Technology, Hyderabad, designed to bring together passionate innovators from across the country to solve real-world problems using technology.

Participants will work in teams through an intense 24-hour innovation sprint, guided by experienced mentors and evaluated by an expert jury. The event focuses on hands-on learning, creativity, teamwork, and practical problem-solving, while encouraging students to think beyond conventional boundaries.
